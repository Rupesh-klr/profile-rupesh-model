# 5. First Client — Vedika Raksha

This profile is the **first client site built on Profilo Designer**.

## About the client

**Vedika Raksha (RV Vedika Raksha)** — *Womb Healing & Fertility Coach | Emotional Healing
Specialist.* She supports women in overcoming the emotional and lifestyle blocks that may affect
their fertility, combining inner-child healing, lifestyle and diet awareness, and guided womb
healing practices. Her audience is primarily **Telugu-speaking women**, with some international
(NRI) clients, and the brand is positioned for homemakers, working women and women entrepreneurs.

## Why this site fits her brand

- **Warm, healing default theme** (rose / peach / plum / gold) — calm, feminine, trustworthy —
  with an elegant dark mode for a premium feel.
- **English + Telugu toggle** — meets the core Telugu audience while staying accessible to
  international clients.
- **Emotional, story-led structure** — leads with the visitor's pain ("trying to conceive but
  nothing is working?"), then challenges → who it's for → what they'll learn → outcomes →
  about/mission → real stories → FAQ → a clear call to action.
- **Trust signals** — animated stats, testimonials with a gentle "results vary" disclaimer, and a
  professional FAQ that clarifies this is supportive (not medical) work.

## Sections used (in order)

1. **Hero** — attention line, headline, workshop highlights (date / time / language / platform),
   portrait.
2. **Stats** — experience, women supported, mission number.
3. **Challenges** (`cardgrid`) — the struggles she helps with.
4. **Audience** (`cardgrid`) — who the work is for.
5. **What You'll Learn** (`curriculum`) — the 3 "secrets".
6. **Outcomes** (`cardgrid`) — how the journey changes.
7. **About + Mission**.
8. **Testimonials**.
9. **FAQ**.
10. **CTA** — next workshop with a live countdown.
